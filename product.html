Ergonomics

We offer more than
comfort and mobility

Our focus is also on the details. We design Elefanto according to the needs of the human body. The ergonomics behind Elefanto is to help users to decompress spine pressure, so that muscle tension and severe back pain are relieved. Furthermore, users can adjust the seat according to their body posture with Elefanto’s deformable mechanism to improve body blood circulation. As a result, users can travel with Elefanto for a long time without feeling stressed.

Operation

Ready for action

On your smartphone, you can control Elefanto via the control panel attached to the armrest or Elefanto-App. The joystick can govern the speed and direction. More detailed information, such as stairs and obstacles, is shown in the App as a reminder.

Design Concept

Extra Protection

The central design concept is to create a form to embrace the user’s body to add stability. The ergonomically designed curve of the seat flows seamlessly along the user’s posture. In addition, the incurvate armrest adds another layer of protection during the journey. Safety is always at the top of our priorities.
